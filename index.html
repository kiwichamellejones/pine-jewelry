About the brand

Every piece carries the character of something discovered.

Our work begins with a love of texture, contrast, and old-world charm. We combine new materials with antiques, reclaimed elements, and thoughtfully selected details to create jewelry that feels storied from the very first wear.

Inspired by pine forests, desert air, and the historic spirit of the Southwest, Pine Jewelry was designed for people who want more than accessories. They want pieces with feeling.
